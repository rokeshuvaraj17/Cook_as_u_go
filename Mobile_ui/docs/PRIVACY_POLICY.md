# Cook As U Go - Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** May 1, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Cook As U Go ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use the Cook As U Go mobile application (the "App").

If you have questions about this policy or your data, contact us at: **[YOUR_CONTACT_EMAIL]**

## 1) Information We Collect

### Account Information
- Email address and account credentials you provide when signing up or logging in.

### Pantry and Bill Data
- Pantry items, quantities, units, notes, bill details, and receipt-derived entries that you create or edit in the App.

### Receipt Images and Scan Data
- If you use receipt scanning, the App may access your camera or photos and process selected images.
- Receipt metadata and extracted line-item information (for example: merchant name, totals, products) may be processed to provide scan and save features.

### Technical Data
- Basic service and diagnostic data such as IP address, timestamps, app version, and server error logs for security and reliability.

### Local Device Storage
- The App may store tokens, preferences, and cached data on your device to keep you signed in and improve performance.

## 2) How We Use Your Information

We use collected data to:
- Create and manage your account
- Provide pantry, bills, and receipt scan features
- Sync your data with backend services
- Maintain security, troubleshoot issues, and improve app reliability
- Comply with legal obligations

## 3) Permissions

The App may request:
- **Camera permission** (`android.permission.CAMERA`) to capture receipt photos
- **Photo/media access** to select receipt images from your device

You can deny or revoke permissions in device settings, but some features (such as receipt scan) may not work.

## 4) Data Sharing

We do **not** sell your personal information.

We may share data with trusted service providers only as needed to operate the App (for example hosting, database, and receipt-processing infrastructure). These providers process data on our behalf and under appropriate safeguards.

We may also disclose data if required by law, legal process, or to protect rights, safety, and security.

## 5) Data Storage and Retention

- Data is processed and stored on backend services used by Cook As U Go.
- We retain data while your account is active and as reasonably necessary for backups, legal compliance, fraud prevention, and dispute resolution.
- You may request deletion of your account data by contacting us.

## 6) Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards, including encrypted transport (HTTPS), to protect your data. No system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 7) Children's Privacy

Cook As U Go is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum required age in your region). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

## 8) Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or export your personal data, and to object to certain processing. Contact us to submit a request.

## 9) International Processing

Your data may be processed in countries where our infrastructure or service providers operate. By using the App, you understand that cross-border data transfers may occur.

## 10) Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will update the "Last updated" date when changes are made. Continued use of the App after updates means you accept the revised policy, where permitted by law.

# Cook As U Go - Account Deletion Policy

**App:** Cook As U Go  
**Developer/Owner:** Rokeshuvaraj  
**Last updated:** May 1, 2026

This page explains how users can request account deletion for Cook As U Go and what data is deleted or retained.

## How to request account deletion

Users can request deletion using either method below:

1. **Email request (primary method)**  
   Send an email from your account email address to: **[YOUR_CONTACT_EMAIL]**  
   Subject: **Account Deletion Request - Cook As U Go**

2. **In-app request (if available in your version)**  
   If your app version includes account deletion in settings/profile, submit the request there.

For security, we may ask you to verify account ownership before processing deletion.

## Deletion timeline

- We start processing verified deletion requests within **7 business days**.
- Account deletion is typically completed within **30 days** of verification.

## What data is deleted

When your account deletion request is completed, we delete or de-identify data associated with your account, including:

- Account profile and authentication linkage
- Pantry and kitchen entries
- Bills and receipt-scan derived entries stored for your account
- App-linked preferences associated with your account in backend storage

## What data may be retained and why

Some limited data may be retained for legal, security, fraud prevention, backup integrity, and operational reasons:

- **Security and server logs** (for abuse prevention and incident investigation)
- **Backup copies** required for disaster recovery cycles
- **Records required by law** (if applicable)

## Retention periods for kept data

- Security/operational logs: up to **90 days**
- Encrypted backup snapshots: up to **30 days** after deletion processing
- Legally required records: retained only for the period required by applicable law

After retention periods expire, retained data is deleted or irreversibly anonymized.

## Important notes

- Uninstalling the app alone does **not** automatically delete your backend account data.
- If you signed up with an email account, submit deletion from that same email for faster verification.
- If you have multiple accounts, submit a separate request for each account email.
